Privacy Policy for FollowMyWord

Effective Date: May 15, 2025

1. Introduction

FollowMyWord is a Unity-based word chain game designed to enhance vocabulary skills and encourage rapid thinking. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when using our app.

2. Data Collection

We only collect the email address provided during registration. This is used solely for saving your gameplay preferences and progress. No sensitive personal information is collected or stored.

3. Data Usage

Collected email addresses are used strictly for user identification and progress tracking within the app. We do not share, sell, or distribute any user data to third parties.

4. Permissions

The app may request permission to access audio features to enable sound effects and music during gameplay. These are used locally and are not transmitted or recorded.

5. Storage and Security

All game data, including word lists and scores, are stored locally on your device. We implement reasonable measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee complete security on third-party platforms.

6. Third-Party Content

The app uses public domain or open-licensed assets for sound and word data. Word meanings are based on WordNet 2.0 and NLTK and are provided as-is for educational use.

7. Children's Privacy

FollowMyWord does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have such data, please contact us.
